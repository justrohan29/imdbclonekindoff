The Shawshank Redemption

Year: 1994

Director: Frank Darabont

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Synopsis: Chronicles the experiences of a banker who is sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary for the murder of his wife and her lover, despite his claims of innocence. During his time at the prison, he befriends a fellow inmate, contrives elaborate money-laundering schemes, and gains the trust and respect of the prison staff and fellow inmates.
